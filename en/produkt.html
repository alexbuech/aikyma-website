The 6 steps · End-to-end pipeline
01 · INTAKE
Monitor inbox
Oskar reads your inbox (Microsoft Graph, Gmail, Exchange), recognizes orders and separates relevant attachments from noise.
02 · CAPTURE
Capture data
AI-based capture of customer data, addresses, line items, quantities, dates and references — from any document format.
03 · MATCH
Match master data
Customers and items are linked to your master data via fuzzy and semantic matching. Previously created quotes are matched automatically.
04 · CHECK
Plausibility check
Configurable business rules are applied: data normalized, exceptions detected, tenant-specific requirements enforced.
05 · RESOLVE
Resolve exceptions
Unclear or risky cases go to your team with targeted questions. Oskar learns continuously from every correction.
06 · HANDOFF
Hand over to ERP
Validated orders directly in Business Central (REST API), SAP (ORDERS05 IDoc) or custom backend. Full audit trail preserved.
Why Oskar
Built for trading reality

No generic AI that occasionally hallucinates. Oskar combines generative AI with heuristics, fuzzy matching and systematic cross-checks — for reproducible, robust and auditable results on every order.

Enterprise features
Production-ready, multi-tenant
  • Tenant-isolated configuration, credentials and data
  • Secret handling via Azure Key Vault
  • Asynchronous processing with persistent state
  • Admin API and CLI for configuration and operations
Human in the loop
People stay in control

Oskar has no decision authority — he recommends and delivers. Unclear cases are routed automatically to your team for approval, including auto-generated clarification questions. Control stays where it belongs.

Master-data sync
Always up to date

Products, customers and references are synchronized continuously from your systems. Oskar never works with outdated data — new items, changed prices and updated delivery addresses are immediately reflected in matching.

The 6 steps · How Petra works
01 · INGEST
Ingest documents & classify
Emails, PDFs, Excel/CSV, Word, images and web URLs — automatically recognized and routed by datasheet or article list.
02 · PORTALS
Web scraping & portal access
Navigates protected supplier portals with 4 authentication methods and cookie consent in 7+ languages.
03 · EXTRACT
AI-powered extraction
Multi-LLM strategy (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) with prompts optimized for German-language content, working across markdown, OCR, images and emails.
04 · ENRICH
Enrich & optimize
Enriched from datasheets and web sources according to your copywriting guidelines. SEO and GEO metadata generated automatically.
05 · QUALITY
Quality & human in the loop
3-stage completeness scoring (max. 13.5 points). Insufficient results are blocked automatically, clarification questions generated.
06 · EXPORT
Export & delivery
Reports in three depths (Parsed, Refined, Enriched) as Excel or customer-specific shop feeds. Up to 39 data fields incl. SEO.
Personality
Versatile, thorough, transparent

Petra processes 20+ document types, 4 authentication methods, 7+ languages — no format is too chaotic. Every source is checked, every contradiction detected, confidence scores and source origin are traceable at any time.

Conservative
When in doubt, block instead of deliver

Petra has no decision authority — she recommends and delivers, doesn't decide. When in doubt, she blocks automatically and escalates to a human. No unchecked data delivery into the production system.

Learning
Every correction becomes a rule

Corrections from the human-in-the-loop flow as new CEL rules into future processing. Petra becomes more precise for your specific requirements with every week.
